About Us

At Cat Haven, we believe in celebrating the beauty, mystery, and charm of cats. From their playful antics to their quiet companionship, cats have a special place in our hearts.

We started as a small group of feline enthusiasts, determined to create a space where every cat lover could come together to share stories, tips, and, of course, pictures of their beloved furballs. Today, we’re proud to be a thriving community dedicated to everything cats.

Whether you’re a long-time cat parent or just starting to learn about these incredible creatures, Cat Haven is here for you. Join us as we explore the world of cats – from adoption advice and care tips to fun facts and heartwarming stories. Together, let’s make the world a better place, one purr at a time.

Meet Our Founder

Our founder, Bharat Harshan , is a lifelong cat lover and advocate. His passion for feline welfare led him to create Cat Haven in 2010. With over a decade of experience in animal care and community building, Bharat has transformed Cat Haven into a haven for cats and their human companions alike.
